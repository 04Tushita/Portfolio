Project Details

Project information

  • Category: Machine Learning
  • Duration: MAR 2022 - MAY 2022
  • Project URL: https://github.com/04Tushita/EVA-Voice-Assistant.git

    • Project Description

    This project introduces a state-of-the-art voice assistant powered by JarvisAI named 'EVA'. This personal assistant enhances your daily life by performing various tasks effortlessly. It greets you warmly, keeps you updated with the latest news, and provides the current date. With a simple voice command, our voice assistant opens websites, launches applications, and retrieves information. It streamlines your tasks, saving you time and effort. Additionally, it can identify the service provider and country associated with a given phone number, offering valuable insights. Driven by advanced natural language processing and machine learning techniques, JarvisAI enables our voice assistant to understand and respond intelligently to a wide range of voice commands. Experience the convenience and efficiency of our voice assistant as it transforms the way you interact with technology.
